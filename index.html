Choose the Version That Makes Sense

there’s only one of me.
but within me there is many.
which would you like to meet?

Choose the version you recognize.

  • The Student – The one who feels safest with something to prove.
  • The Driven One – The one who doesn’t like standing still.
  • The Easygoing One – The one who says it’s fine.
  • The Quiet One – The one who notices more than she says.
  • The Put-Together One – The one people don’t worry about.
