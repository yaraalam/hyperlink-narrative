The Imposter

if you follow the threads long enough, they all seem to end here.

sometimes it feels like everyone else knows something i’m still pretending to understand.

and if you look closely enough, you’ll see i am performing.
not lying.
just adjusting.
always adjusting.

each version of me makes sense on its own.

none of them are false.

but sometimes i wonder if i’ve gotten too good at arranging them.
i’ve become very good at giving people the version of me that makes sense to them.

sometimes i forget which version feels like mine.

maybe you'd want to choose again?
